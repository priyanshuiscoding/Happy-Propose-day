💌 A Letter for You

From my heart to yours, Soumya

My Dearest Soumya,

In the quiet of my thoughts, I find myself writing to you —
not because I have anything new to say,
but because my heart never tires of saying it again.

You came like a gentle storm,

Shaking my world and calming it at once.

In your eyes I found home,

In your smile I found peace,

In your presence I found myself.


We fight, we laugh, we cry —

But through it all, one thing remains:

My love for you grows stronger every day.

Like the moon that never leaves the sky,

You will always have a place in my heart.


I don't know what the future holds,

But I know I want to hold your hand through it all.

Be my better half, Soumya —

Today, tomorrow, and forever.

This letter may end here, but my love for you never will. You are the best thing that has ever happened to me. 💕

With all my love, forever,
Yours only 💗
